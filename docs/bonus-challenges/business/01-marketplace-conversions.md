## The Problem

## Things to Consider

### Grid ‚Üí Detail Page (Click-through)

- What makes a card compelling enough to click?
- Is critical information visible or hidden?
- Does the grid help users find relevant listings?
- Are there enough visual cues and hierarchy?

### Detail Page ‚Üí Booking (Conversion)

- Does the page answer buyer questions?
- Is the value proposition clear?
- What objections might prevent booking?
- Is the CTA prominent and compelling?

### Overall Experience

- How does the flow feel end-to-end?
- Where is there unnecessary friction?
- What's missing that users would expect?
- How do you build trust and urgency?
